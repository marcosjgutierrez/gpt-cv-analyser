Senior JavaScript Developer

Job Description:
We are looking for a Senior JavaScript Developer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, testing, and debugging responsive web and mobile applications for the company. 

Responsibilities:
- Design the front end architecture
- Develop and enhance our main front end platform - website.
- Develop new user-facing features.
- Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
- Maintain updated knowledge of the development industry and any advancements in technology.
- Work with a team of developers to design, develop, unit test, and support new features.
- Strive for high quality and rapid output through the use of agile software development best practices.
- Perform code reviews and enhance collaboration.
- Communicate with end users, developers, and product managers to ensure efficient design and delivery.

Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
- 5+ years of experience in JavaScript development.
- Proficiency in front-end development web programming languages such as HTML and CSS, JQuery, and JavaScript.
- Proficient understanding of client-side scripting and JavaScript frameworks, including jQuery.
- Experience with modern JavaScript libraries and tooling.
- Commanding grasp of HTML, CSS, and related web technologies.
- Strong understanding of responsive web design techniques.
- Understanding of cross-browser compatibility issues.
- Strong problem-solving skills.
- Excellent verbal communication skills.
- Good interpersonal skills.
